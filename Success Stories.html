1.Alex Smith

From Beginner to PC Builder expert
Alex was gamer but had little to no knowledge about how PCs actually worked. Frustrated by constant performance issues with pre-built machines, Alex decided to join our Academy to learn how to build and customize his own PC ,Through our "pc academy course", Alex gained a deep understanding of PC components, assembly, and optimization. He started building custom PCs for friends and family, even launching a side business offering personalized builds.

Success:

Today, Alex is the owner of a small PC-building business. His expertise has led him to work with local gaming tournaments, providing tech support and building high-performance rigs for esports professionals.

2.Sarah Johnson

Turning Passion into a Career
Sarah had always been interested in technology but was working in retail, unsure how to break into the tech industry. After discovering PC Components Academy, she realized that she could turn her passion for tech into a fulfilling career. Sarah enrolled in our workshop, learning everything from diagnosing faulty hardware to replacing key components like CPUs and GPUs. Her hands-on experience in the workshop gave her the confidence to pursue a career in IT support.

Success:

After completing the program, Sarah landed a job as a technical support specialist at a major tech company. Within a year, she was promoted to lead technician.She's now able to troubleshoot and repair a wide range of hardware, helping thousands of customers.
